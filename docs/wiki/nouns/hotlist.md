# Hotlist

A priority-tiered notification system: Tier 1 (mentions, bold+count), Tier 2 (DMs, bold), Tier 3 (unread, bold), Tier 4 (activity, normal). Alt+A cycles through mentions first; read-marker separator shows last-read position.
